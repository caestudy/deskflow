## Features

**Focus Zone** — Make the app come to you.
DeskFlow defines a focus zone — your primary field of view where your active app always lands. ⌘Tab to anything and it flows there.

**UltraFocus** — Disappear into the work.
Dims the entire screen — menu bar, dock, everything — leaving only the one app in your focus zone. One surface, one task, less noise.

**Multi-Display** — Control all of your displays.
Zones are defined per display. Layouts restore on reconnect. Apps can be exempted from zone management when you need them to roam free.

**Low Overhead** — Zero interference. Nothing to unlearn.
Runs on ⌘Tab. Additional hotkeys live in a dedicated ⌃⌘ namespace, chosen not to conflict with your terminal, Xcode, VS Code, Raycast, and Rectangle.
